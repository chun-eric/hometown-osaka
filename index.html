Top Activities in Osaka

Attractions

Osaka Castle

Explore Osaka Castle, a symbol of Japan's past. Built over 400 years ago, its strong walls and golden details tell stories of samurai and ancient battles. Wander through peaceful gardens and enjoy stunning city views. A visit to Osaka Castle is a step back in time.

arrow_forward_ios close

Eating

Dotonbori

Indulge in the flavors of Dotonbori, Osaka's famous food district. Savor iconic street foods like takoyaki, okonomiyaki, and kushikatsu as you wander the neon-lit streets. With every bite, experience the vibrant spirit and culinary magic that make Dotonbori a must-visit.

arrow_forward_ios close

Shopping

Shinsaibashi

Discover Shinsaibashi, Osaka's premier shopping district, where traditional and modern collide. Stroll through the bustling arcade filled with trendy boutiques, luxury brands, and unique local shops. Whether you're hunting for fashion, or souvenirs, Shinsaibashi has it all.
